Hi! I am Anirudh K.M. Kakarlapudi. I envision myself exploring various facets of Artificial Intelligence, especially in NLP and Machine Learning in the real world. I also love electronics and experiment with different circuits in my free time.

I am currently pursuing Masters in Artificial Intelligence under the guidance of [Dr. Prashant Doshi](https://cs.uga.edu/directory/people/prashant-doshi) at the University of Georgia, Athens, GA. As a Graduate Research Assistant at [THINC LAB](http://thinc.cs.uga.edu/), my research is in the domain of Communicative Interactive Partial Observable Markov Decision Processes(C-IPOMDPS). I am developing a Decision Planning algorithm in open and multi-agent systems where agents with constrained communication can enter and leave the system to achieve a joint task. I did my bachelor's in Electronics and Communication at Andhra University, India, back in 2017.
